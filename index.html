Why does one play Fusiondartz?

As entertainment, or to settle arguments. Or wars.


The Rules of Fusiondartz

All participants have to suck at darts. Don't fake it.

Who starts is decided by the person that throws closest to, or in the bulls-eye. Repeat every set.

Your goal is to reach 100. Easy right? No it's not if you suck at darts.

Doubles and triples count but are not required to finish a set.

You decide on number of sets and games. We recommend best of three sets.

Throw 69 and you win the set and start the next one. You can't aim to hit 69.

Remember, Fusiondartz is a gentleman's game.

Final, but most important rule:

Fusiondartz must be played with a beer.
